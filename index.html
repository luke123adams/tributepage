Ozzy Osbourne

The Prince of Darkness

Here's an outline of important moments in Ozzy's life:

  • 1948 - Born in Marston Green Maternity Hospital in Birmingham.
  • 1963 - Leaves school at the age of 15 and has a string of odd jobs including construction worker, car horn tuner and abbattoir worker. Decides he wants to become a musician after hearing The Beatles.
  • 1967 - Geezer Butler forms his first band, Rare Breed, and recruits Ozzy as vocalist. The band break up after 2 shows.
  • 1968 - Ozzy places an advert in a local music shop: "Ozzy Zigg needs gig- has own PA." He and Geezer Butler are enlisted by guitarist Tony Iommi and drummer Bill Ward to start a new band called Earth.
  • 1969 - After the band get repeatedly mistaken for another group of the same name, they decide to change their name to Black Sabbath after a 1963 horror film playing at the cinema across the road from their recording studio.
  • 1970 - Black Sabbath release their eponymous debut album on Friday 13th February, recorded over 2 days at Trident studios. It is critically panned but a commercial success.
  • 1971-1977 - The band release a number of albums throughout the 1970s and Ozzy suffers from a prolonged period of despair and drug abuse.
    • 1979 - Ozzy is kicked out of the band because of his drug abuse. While living in Los Angeles Ozzy is signed to Jet Records as a solo artist by Don Arden, who sends his daughter Sharon to "look after Ozzy's needs and protect his investment".
  • 1980 - Ozzy releases his first solo album, Blizzard of Ozz. The album attracts controversy and Ozzy is sued by the parents of a depressed teenager who committed suicide, blaming one his songs for influencing their son's actions.
  • 1982 - Ozzy marries Sharon Levy. Ozzy's guitarist and close friend, Randy Rhoads, is killed in an airplane crash while on tour. Ozzy falls into a deep depression.
  • 1996 - After a string of solo albums, Ozzy's wife creates the first Ozzfest music festival.
  • 2002 - Ozzy's new reality tv show, 'The Osbournes' premieres on MTV
  • 2003 - Ozzy is rushed to hospital after seriously injuring himself on a quad bike. While he is recovering, he achieves his first ever UK number 1 single for the song Changes, a duet with his daughter, Kelly.
  • 2011 - Ozzy, Iommi, Butler and Ward announce the reunion of the original Black Sabbath lineup.
  • 2012 - Black Sabbath play their first live concert since 2005 at the 02 Academy in Bimingham
  • 2022 - Ozzy and Tony Iommi make a surprise appearance at the closing ceremony of the Commonwealth games in Birmingham.
